About Hundru Falls

Hundru Falls is one of the most spectacular waterfalls in Jharkhand, cascading from a height of approximately 98 meters (320 feet). Created by the Subarnarekha River, this magnificent waterfall is located about 45 kilometers from Ranchi and stands as one of the highest waterfalls in the state.

The falls are particularly impressive during the monsoon season when the water volume is at its peak, creating a thunderous roar as it plunges into the pool below. The surrounding landscape is adorned with lush green vegetation and rocky terrain, creating a perfect backdrop for this natural wonder. The mist created by the falling water provides a refreshing experience, especially during the hot summer months.

The area around Hundru Falls offers excellent opportunities for adventure activities such as rock climbing, rappelling, and trekking. The rocky outcrops around the falls provide challenging climbing routes for adventure enthusiasts. The natural pool at the base of the falls is perfect for a refreshing dip, though visitors should exercise caution during the monsoon season when the water current is strong.
